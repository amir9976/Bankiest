100% digital bank

Bankist is a 100 digital bank that offers customers an easy, convenient way to manage their finances. With Bankist, customers can keep track of their spending, set up budgeting goals, and transfer money between accounts with ease. Plus, Bankist’s mobile app makes it easy for customers to bank on the go. Whether you’re looking for a simple way to manage your finances or you’re looking for a full-fledged digital banking experience, Bankist has you covered.

Watch your money grow

Bankist is an app that will allow you to see your money grow over time. This is a great way to stay on top of your finances and make sure that your money is working for you. The app will show you how much money you have saved, how much you have invested, and how much your money has grown over time.

Free debit card included

If you're looking for a new bank, you should definitely check out Bankist. Not only do they offer great rates on savings and checking accounts, but they also give you a free debit card! That's right - with Bankist, you can get all the benefits of a traditional bank without having to pay any fees.
